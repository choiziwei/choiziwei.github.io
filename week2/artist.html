Mrs. GREEN APPLE

Biography

Mrs. GREEN APPLE is a highly acclaimed Japanese rock band formed in Tokyo in 2013. They made their major debut in 2015 and quickly rose to prominence with their vibrant musical style that perfectly blends energetic rock, synth-pop, and orchestral elements. The band is currently composed of three members: Motoki Ohmori (vocals and guitar), Hiroto Wakai (guitar), and Ryoka Fujisawa (keyboards). They are particularly famous for their incredibly catchy melodies and the extraordinary, expressive vocal range of their lead singer, Ohmori. Their lyrics often explore deeply emotional themes of youth, hope, and personal struggles, resonating with a massive audience. After a brief hiatus, they returned in 2022 stronger than ever, releasing multiple chart-topping hits and contributing iconic theme songs to popular anime series and movies. Today, Mrs. GREEN APPLE is recognized as one of the most prominent and dynamic acts in the modern Japanese music scene.
